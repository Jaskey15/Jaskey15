# Jacob Askey

Biological engineer turned Peace Corps volunteer turned software builder. Now I'm back at a keyboard, building systems that act on your behalf rather than just responding to commands.

Writing about technology, travel, and the human experience at [jacobaskey.com](https://www.jacobaskey.com).
